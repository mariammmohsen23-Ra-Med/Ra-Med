# About the project
Ra-MED Health Tourism was founded to position Egypt as a leading destination for medical and wellness travel. Inspired by Ra: the ancient Egyptian symbol of life and renewal, and Med: representing modern healthcare. The brand combines Egypt’s healing heritage with contemporary medical excellence. Ra-MED provides international patients with trusted medical services, seamless travel coordination, and personalized care throughout their treatment journey.

# Vision
To position Egypt as a leading global destination for safe, high-quality, and culturally rich medical tourism, where world class healthcare meets the warmth and legacy of the sun.
# Mission
Ra-Med connects international patients with Egypt’s top medical institutions, providing seamless, transparent, and personalized healthcare experiences -from consultation to recovery- with comfort, safety, and care at the heart of every journey.
